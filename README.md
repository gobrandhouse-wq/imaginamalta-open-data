# ImaginaMalta Open Travel Data Sources

ImaginaMalta is a structured Malta travel data project that maps localities, hotels, beaches, viewpoints, landmarks, festas, diving wrecks and travel guides.

The public methodology and source overview is available here:

https://imaginamalta.com/imaginamalta-open-data/

## What the dataset covers

ImaginaMalta currently organises source-backed information about:

* Malta and Gozo localities
* Hotels and licensed stays
* Verified beaches
* Viewpoints
* Landmarks and heritage sites
* Diving wrecks
* Festa calendar pages
* Film locations
* Travel guides
* Near-hotel travel pages

## Data quality approach

Not every collected record is published or indexed.

ImaginaMalta uses quality gates to separate:

* verified records that can be indexable,
* source-backed records that may remain noindex until reviewed,
* imported records that need data completeness checks,
* thin or incomplete pages that should stay outside the sitemap.

This helps avoid fake data, duplicate sitemap URLs, noindex/sitemap contradictions and unsupported travel claims.
